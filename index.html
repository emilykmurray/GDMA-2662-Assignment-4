Our Philosophy

Yes, Yoga is becoming quite trendy and as many become more open to the physical practices of yoga in the west more and more studios are coming in the scene. On the West coast it almost seems to be on every corner and the East is catching up. It's for sure a great time for those who enjoy the practice, but our particular motive in starting Good Vibes Yoga & Kombucha bar was not a product of popularity, trends, or money, we really believe in yoga!

It was a realization of the community and benefits such a practice could provide for an individual and the idea of how rewarding giving those same benefits to others would feel. Instructor, Mike Giaimo for example, whos life experience brought him to a place where he had difficult times realizing his capabilities, seeing the glass half full, started to lose purpose in his career in business, and even much of his faith.

Class Levels

1

Restorative

Normal room temperature, supported and slower paced, guided explanations, avoiding advances postures. Great for first timers.

2

Warm Vinyasa

85-88 degree temperature. Has more pace and attempts some challenging postures. Open level & definitely fine if you've practiced before.

3

Hot Vinyasa

95-105 degree temperature. Has pace, more detail & challenging postures. Inlcuding arm balances and active inveresions.

Kombucha Bar

Kombucha is fermented probiotic tea which contains live culture that is said to be beneficial for your digestive system as well as good as a defense for bad bacteria's that can cause infection or illness. It has vitamin B to give energy, and also benefits your gut. Often flavored with fruit, spice, or herbs to give them all unique flavors and often effervescent.

Yes, it has many benefits but we love the way it tastes & and it often tastes even better right after a yoga class enjoying it with friends. We choose the most simply produced and what we feel are the best tasting Kombucha's and serve them in bottles and on tap for practitioners to enjoy before or after practices. Our bar also offers a variety of teas, Virgin kombucha based cocktails (or Mocktails), snacks, and more to replenish and satisfy oneself or enjoy a conversation over after class.
